Welcome to CristO

CristO is a comprehensive enterprise resource planning (ERP) software designed for church-related and not-for-profit organizations. This cloud-based solution caters to the specific needs of dioceses, religious institutes, apostolic organizations (such as educational, healthcare, social service, media, and retreat centers), as well as parishes, families, and individual members. CristO offers four distinct products: Parokia for parish management, Religio for religious institutes, Cathedra for diocesan administration, and Charisma for denominational church management.

Province Management Software

Welcome to Religio

A paperless technology driver back office for an administration. This user-friendly software tool is designed for the religious head of an institute, province secretary, community heads, and all province members. It allows for the management of communications (circulars, emails, meetings), creation of records (chronicles, concerns), and maintenance of critical and personal member information. The software also supports tracking assignments, history, institutional/community information, planning, reporting, and archiving.
